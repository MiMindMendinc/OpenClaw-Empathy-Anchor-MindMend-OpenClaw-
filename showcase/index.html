Safety boundaries

Say what this is. Say what it is not.

This is

  • A supportive safety prototype
  • A deterministic demo scanner
  • Informational crisis resource routing
  • Local-first infrastructure for sensitive demos

This is not

  • Clinical software or a medical device
  • An emergency service or hotline
  • Therapy or diagnostic care
  • A replacement for parents, clinicians, or 988/911

If someone may be in immediate danger, call or text 988 or contact emergency services.
